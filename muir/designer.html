Tactile Design - Lindsey M. Muir - MFA Graphic Design Thesis 2009

Originally from Los Angeles, Lindsey M. Muir received her bachelor of arts degree in graphic design from the University of Connecticut. Prior to returning to graduate school, Lindsey worked at an advertising agency where she designed marketing campaigns for real estate developers.

Lindsey enjoyed designing for the blind and hopes to continue working with organizations that would benefit from her efforts.

When Lindsey isn't designing, she enjoys coming up with ideas to rid the world of bad design. She intends to open her own design studio in southern California in the future.
