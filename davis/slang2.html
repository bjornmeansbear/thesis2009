Size: 5x2.5x5, skewed block

The final form, toy blocks, are heavily skewed to visually represent the unconventional content development. The assembled blocks, when placed on their sides, sit at such
an angle as to also inhibit stacking. This utterly useless tool serves one purpose: to teach negativity in the most charming way possible.
