Typeface: Elephants & Castles

The most unique custom lettering, Elephants and Castles, provided enough inspiration
and versatility to warrant further expansion. Developing A-Z, or rather a-z, had a fair amount of snags but, in the end, the lowercase characters finished strong.

Size: 4x4, card set

To test if it’s a “good” typeface, how well
does it make a face from its characters?




The card set, featuring faces of type from
butchered letterforms, introduces the typeface
to the world with a little flair.
