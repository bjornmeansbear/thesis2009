About Me





I recieved my BFA at Cameron University in Lawton, Oklahoma (don't
hold it against me). During my stay there, I developed a love of design
that propelled me forward into Grad School at MICA. I began hand-
lettering and illustrating for projects at MICA and I've grown to really enjoy the process and, of course, the final results. It's here that I've
finally discovered my own design identity and now I'm excited to see where I go and what work gets me famous. Or at least gets me happy.
