LETTERING STUDY






Globe used graphic devices, color and dynamic layouts to create chunks of information resulting in layouts that could be quickly scanned by the eye. Since photos are unidentifiable at a distance, Globe kept them small to allow for larger names. Some performers appreciated this stylistic approach, as each act had its own moment on the poster regardless of popularity.
