THE HAND OF HARRY KNORR

Harry Knorr’s hand lettering countered Globe’s wooden typography. Knorr could execute several typefaces by memory and numerous additional styles from reference. During his nearly 50 years as the in-house designer/artist, Knorr created artwork, graphics and layouts for everything from billboards to posters.

These specimens explore the wide range of Knorr’s show poster lettering.
